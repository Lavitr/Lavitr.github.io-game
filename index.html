Description of the game

Mean aliens landed on our planet. They have scared the shit out of birds . And birds literally started to ease themself too frequently. Aliens put a spell on a lovely creature - dragon Drakosha . Previously he used to be a vegan and ate potatoes only , but now Drakosha become a real predator hunting for a human flesh. Small Britney got pissed off by everything and started to kill an invaders . Your task to kill as many wicked dwarfs as you can . So hopefully Drakosha will return to his vegan lifestyle and birds mental state will get better.

RULES

You can kill an alien from the gun.

If there is no bullets left you still can kill by knife.Spare bullets appear from time to time.Try to collect them.

If you hit by arrow you lose one life

You can't kill the bird or the dragon,because they are still nice creatures. To escape from dragon try to get down .

If 'SHIT HAPPENS' free time you lose one life. Try to jump over it.You can jump over the alien as well.
